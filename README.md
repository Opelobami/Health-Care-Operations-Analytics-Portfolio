# Project Overview
A curated collection of Health & Care Operations Analytics Projects demonstrating skills in data cleaning, modeling, visualization, and business insight generation.

Operational data in care settings carries more weight than most; a length-of-stay metric isn't just a number, it represents real time, real resources, and real outcomes for the people or animals a system is responsible for.

This portfolio takes that seriously. The projects here are built to help care and welfare organizations see where their processes are working, where they're quietly failing, and what a smarter allocation of limited resources could look like in practice.

## 🧩 Featured Projects

### 🩺Healthy Life Hospital  

A normalized SQL database simulating hospital operations covering patient admissions, diagnoses, GP visits, and ward management. The goal is to build a system that supports **clinical decision-making**, **operational efficiency**, and **data-driven hospital management**.
